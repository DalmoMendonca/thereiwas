# Three-minute demo script

Hold this recording until the final product is approved.

## 0:00 to 0:20

Open `https://thereiwas.dalmo.ai` in a private window.

"Google Timeline has years of my location data. I wanted to press Play. There I Was finds the trips in that export and rebuilds the route so I can watch the whole thing again."

Click **Try with Sample Data**.

## 0:20 to 0:45

Point to the three trips.

"These are pared-down records from my own Timeline: California, New York, and Italy. The same worker and trip detector handle a normal upload. No fictional vacation, no duplicate date ranges, and no generic titles."

Open **California**.

## 0:45 to 1:25

Let the route finish preparing, then press **Play**.

"This was a road trip from Oklahoma to California and back. Sparse Google samples go through Mapbox Directions, recorded paths keep their original geometry, and missing joins are rebuilt. The map fits the complete loop. One clock controls the moving marker, the route behind it, local time, travel mode, and photo moments."

Let the marker advance far enough for the trailing line to be obvious. Scrub once, then return to the start.

## 1:25 to 1:50

Add two or three geotagged photos prepared for the demo.

"The browser reads each photo's capture time and GPS metadata. The files stay on this device. Selecting a photo jumps to its moment in the trip, and playback brings the matching image into view when the marker reaches it."

Select one thumbnail and point to its map marker and synchronized viewport.

## 1:50 to 2:35

Click **Create story**.

"GPT-5.6 gets a bounded dossier with named places, dates, travel modes, and summarized movement. It gets no coordinates, home location, raw path points, source file, or photos. Structured Outputs give me editable chapters and questions. The prompt forbids invented companions, purpose, emotion, weather, and events."

Show the generated notice, chapter names, and one reflection question. Answer the question if the response time leaves room.

## 2:35 to 2:55

Return to **All trips** and open the imported-data disclosure.

"The Timeline, routes, edits, photos, and story drafts live in IndexedDB. I can delete all of it from here."

## 2:55 to 3:00

Return to the California trip and leave the full loop on screen.

"The data remembers where I was. This gives me a way back into the trip."
